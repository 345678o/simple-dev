About Us

Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) at NITI Aayog is mandated to promote a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in India. AIM has selected a number of institutions for supporting them in establishing the Atal Community Innovation Centers (ACICs). CBIT has been selected from Telangana by AIM and received approval for the establishment of Atal Community Innovation Centre (ACIC) at CBIT Campus. ACIC-CBIT provides requisite infrastructure and resources for innovators in underserved regions.

Our Vision

Encourage entrepreneurship in underserved regions by providing infrastructure and innovation support.

Our Mission

Provide mentorship, infrastructure, and frameworks to develop innovators into sustainable entrepreneurs.

Our Objectives

1. Build innovative and entrepreneurial mindsets in the community.
2. Enhance domain knowledge, skills, and attitudes for innovation.
3. Promote design thinking and prototype development.
4. Build a strong startup ecosystem through collaboration.
5. Provide mentor networks and expert guidance.
6. Offer legal, IPR, and funding support for startups.
