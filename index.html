About ACIC-CBIT

Empowering Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) at NITI Aayog is mandated to promote a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in India. AIM has selected a number of institutions for establishing Atal Community Innovation Centers (ACICs). CBIT has been selected from Telangana by AIM for setting up an Atal Community Innovation Centre (ACIC) at CBIT Campus.

ACIC-CBIT Research and Entrepreneurship Foundation, a registered Section 8 company, serves as a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) promoted by CBIT. We provide essential infrastructure and resources for innovators, particularly in unserved and underserved regions.

Our center engages grassroot innovators, communities, students, and academicians in various innovation and entrepreneurship activities, including ideation, problem-solving, Proof of Concept development, Design Thinking, and IPR. ACIC-CBIT actively connects with stakeholders across multiple platforms to accelerate the implementation of innovative solutions.

Our Vision

ACIC CBIT vision is to encourage a spirit of entrepreneurship in the underserved/unserved regions by providing enabling infrastructure and facilitating an environment for innovations.

Our Mission

To provide directional training and mentorship with infrastructural and Adoptive Innovation frameworks for the innovators to offer cost-effective, sustainable solutions for societal problems by targeting to achieve Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Innovation Center will serve as an operational knowledge and resource catalyst for the Innovators to develop into promising entrepreneurs to address the emergent need of the community by providing local innovative and scalable solutions.

Our Objectives

ACIC CBIT vision is to encourage a spirit of entrepreneurship in the underserved/unserved regions by providing enabling infrastructure and facilitating an environment for innovations

To build an Innovative mindset and the need for entrepreneurship among the community.
To enhance the domain knowledge, skills and attitudes (K-S-A) to develop innovative products and services.
To promote design thinking processes among the community and provide the necessary infrastructure support for building prototypes and MVP.
To build a vibrant startup ecosystem, by establishing a network between academia, industries, incubation centers, financial institutions, Government and other stakeholders.
To build the sector specific Expert Mentor pool from academia and industry to provide expert opinions and suggestions to Innovators/startups.
To provide necessary support services, such as Legal, IPR, Funding and Market Access to help innovators become sustainable startups

Targeted SDG's
