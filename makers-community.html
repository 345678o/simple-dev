Makers Community at ACIC- MLRIT

An innovative platform dedicated to fostering a dynamic community of passionate makers committed to product development. Our initiative thrives on promoting collaboration among makers, innovators, and industries to elevate both processes and products. This community comes together to support grassroots innovators in helping them build marketable products. Through our DIG-IN (Develop, Innovate & Grow through Industry Nexus) initiative, we actively bridge the gap between students and industries. This synergy leverages fresh student perspectives to identify shortcomings in industrial processes and contribute to potential solutions. Makers within our community play a crucial role in product development and technical documentation, particularly for grassroots innovators and products intended for technology transfer.
Who can join?
Students, Faculty, lab technicians, enthusiasts, and professionals passionate about product development, innovation, and collaboration.
Why join?
Engage in a vibrant community, bridge academia and industry, contribute to new products, aid technical documentation, and connect with like-minded innovators.
Benefits
Support grassroots innovators, gain valuable industry insights, play a critical role in technology transfer, and build a strong professional network.
